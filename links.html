What can I find here?

This is a page with resources that can help make a lesson more fun, some of the activities are just for part of a lesson, some are full lesson plans. Please review the materials and use how you see fit, if you can think of something that will improve the plans that I have created, please share on my contact page.

Links

  • Flo-Joe

    Flo-Joe is an excellent site if you are looking for resources or activities related to exam work, it covers all levels and has tasks related to all major exams.

  • Lesson Stream

    Lesson Stream covers some really fun topics, and most of the lessons will take an hour. The lesson "Message in a Bottle" is a great lesson to do before the students do a writing exam.

  • Breaking News English

    The lessons on Breaking News English, a based on current news topic, you will find a lot of interesting topics, be careful choosing the lessons, as the topics are what is in the news, so there could be sensitivity issues.

  • British Council

    The British Council caters to all levels, you will find games and activities cover a lot of the vocabulary and grammar needed by the students
