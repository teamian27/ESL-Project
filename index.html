ESL RESOURCES

The home of fun learning lessons

What can I find here?

This is a page with resources that can help make a lesson more fun, some of the activities are just for part of a lesson, some are full lesson plans. Please review the materials and use how you see fit, if you can think of something that will improve the plans that I have created, please share on my contact page.

Lessons created

The majority of the resources available on this site are aimed at B1 (intermediate) up, you can modify some of the resources for lower levels. I have used all these resources with intermediate and higher successfully, the lessons are fun, and you may have to review vocabulary before you start any lesson.

All resources on this site are created by me, some are original, some are modified from ideas I have found elsewhere, please do as you want with my resouces.
